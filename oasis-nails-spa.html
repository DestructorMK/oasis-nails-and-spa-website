Our story

A space designed
for quiet luxury.

Oasis Nails & Spa was founded with a single conviction: that a nail appointment should feel like a genuine respite — not a rushed transaction. We designed every detail of the experience around that belief.

Our studio is kept intentionally calm. Our team is small, and we keep it that way. Each artist is selected for their technical precision and their ability to make every client feel like they're the only one in the room.

Immaculate Standards
Hospital-grade sterilization on all tools. Single-use files and buffers, always.
Curated Products
We carry only clean, professional-grade brands that perform — and last.
Expert Artistry
Every artist holds advanced certifications. Ongoing education is required.
Your Pace
Appointments are never stacked. Your time is respected, start to finish.

Before you visit

Booking
policy.

Arrival

Please arrive 5 minutes before your appointment time. This allows us to ensure your experience begins without feeling rushed — and that your artist can give you their full attention from the start.

Cancellation

Cancellations require at least 24 hours' notice. Late arrivals may result in a shortened service. We appreciate your understanding — this protects the experience of every client we serve.

Group Bookings

A credit card is required to hold group bookings of 3 or more guests. We love hosting groups — we simply ask for a small hold to ensure your reservation is protected. Please contact us directly to arrange.
