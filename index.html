Our story

A space designed
for quiet luxury.

Oasis Nails & Spa was created with the customers' health and comfort in mind in every step of the service. Every service is done with care and precision. Here, everyone belongs. Come join us in our serene space, where you can relax and let us take care of you.

Our studio is kept intentionally calm, with massage chairs designed for maximum comfort and even kids chairs for your little ones. Our team is small, and we keep it that way. Each artist is selected for their technical precision and their ability to make every client feel like they're the only one in the room.

Before you visit

Booking
policy.

Arrival

Please arrive 5 minutes before your appointment time. This allows us to ensure your experience begins without feeling rushed - and that your artist can give you their full attention from the start.

Cancellation

Cancellations require at least 24 hours' notice. Late arrivals may result in a shortened service. We appreciate your understanding - this protects the experience of every client we serve.

No Refund

We're here to help you look and feel beautiful - though supplies, labour, and other costs mean we're unable to offer refunds. Instead, we're happy to correct any mistakes or concerns.

Accidentally smudged your new nails? Come back within 5 days (or 2 days for regular polish) of your original service date for a free fix!
